Our Donate Plan

We want people around us and young children not to be deprived of any of their basic rights

Food For a Family

Among the basic human rights, we value their food first. Many people are living on just one meal at a time so just a few of your donations can make up for their lack of food.

Shelter For a Family

Ensuring people's living space is also our main goal. Many people are currently homeless, sleeping on roads, pavements, railway stations and under trees because they have no place to stay.

Education For a Child

A child’s right to education entails the right to learn. Yet, for too many children across the globe, schooling does not lead to learning.So some of your donations can make a child an educated person
